3  Fasting, Body Composition, and Lifestyle Patterns by Age Group

3.1 Analysis Objective

This report explores how fasting measurements, BMI, body size, sex, and lifestyle-related variables vary across two age groups:

  • Age <= 30
  • Age > 30

The objective is to identify descriptive patterns, compare distributions, and examine how anthropometric and metabolic variables behave across these two age-based segments.
